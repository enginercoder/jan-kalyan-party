About Us

A Party Built on People's Welfare

Jan Kalyan Party was founded with a singular vision — to put development first and rise above the divisions of caste and religion. Under the leadership of Mr. Lal Bahadur Pal, we are committed to transparent governance, inclusive growth, and the welfare of every citizen.

Our Agenda

Our Core Pillars

Six foundational commitments that guide every decision, policy, and action of Jan Kalyan Party.

🏗️
Infrastructure Development
Building roads, schools, hospitals, and digital infrastructure to connect every corner of India.
📚
Quality Education
Ensuring free, quality education for every child, empowering the next generation of leaders.
🏥
Healthcare for All
Affordable healthcare, mental wellness, and modern medical facilities in every district.
💼
Employment & Economy
Creating sustainable jobs, supporting small businesses, and boosting local economies.
👩‍🌾
Farmers' Welfare
Fair prices, modern farming support, and financial security for every farming family.
♀️
Women Empowerment
Equal rights, safety, and opportunities for women in every field of society.
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🇮🇳
Nationalism
India first — every policy, every action serves the nation and its people.
⚖️
Equality
No discrimination based on caste, religion, gender, or region. One India, One People.
🔍
Transparency
Open governance, accountable leadership, and honest communication with citizens.
🤲
Seva (Service)
Public service is our duty. We serve the people — not ourselves.
