Who We Are

A Movement for Real Change

Jan Kalyan Party (JKP) is a political party founded on the belief that true progress comes only when development is prioritised over divisive politics. We stand firmly against caste-based and religion-based vote bank politics and pledge to serve every citizen equally.

Our slogan — "जात पर न पात पर, प्राथमिकता विकास पर" — is not just words. It is the foundation of everything we do. From policy to grassroots action, we remain committed to inclusive development that leaves no one behind.

Founded by Mr. Lal Bahadur Pal, the party has rapidly grown into a people's movement with thousands of members, volunteers, and supporters across India.

Message from Our President

"जात पर न पात पर, प्राथमिकता विकास पर — यही हमारी पार्टी का मूल मंत्र है। हम एक ऐसे भारत का निर्माण करना चाहते हैं जहाँ हर नागरिक को समान अवसर मिले, जहाँ विकास ही सबसे बड़ी राजनीति हो।" — Mr. Lal Bahadur Pal, Founder & President, Jan Kalyan Party

Mr. Lal Bahadur Pal founded Jan Kalyan Party with a vision to create a political movement rooted in the welfare of the common people. With deep grassroots connections and a commitment to transparent, development-focused governance, he has dedicated his life to public service. Under his leadership, the party has grown into a credible force for change, standing for the rights and welfare of every Indian regardless of their background.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯
Our Mission

To build a transparent, inclusive, and development-focused political movement that puts the welfare of every Indian citizen — regardless of caste, religion, or economic background — at the heart of governance.

Prioritise development over divisive politics
Empower marginalised communities through policy
Ensure transparent, accountable governance
Build grassroots political awareness across India
🔭
Our Vision

A prosperous, equal, and united India where every citizen has access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunity — and where political decisions are driven by data, compassion, and national interest.

100% literacy and quality education nationwide
Universal healthcare accessible to all citizens
Zero unemployment through skill & industry growth
A corruption-free, digital India for all
Our Journey

Party History

2024
Party Founded
Jan Kalyan Party officially founded by Mr. Lal Bahadur Pal with the mission to bring development-first politics to India.
2024
First 1,000 Members
Within months of founding, the party registered its first 1,000 members and established local units across multiple districts.
2024
Volunteer Programme Launched
The JKP Volunteer Programme was launched, mobilising hundreds of youth volunteers for community welfare activities.
2025
Digital Expansion
Launch of the official JKP website with online membership, digital ID cards, and the member portal for all registered members.
Future
National Presence
Expanding to all 28 states and 8 Union Territories — building the largest development-focused political movement in India.
