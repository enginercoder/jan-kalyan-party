How to Become an Active Member?
Active Membership is not applied for directly. Every new member starts as a General Member and gets automatically upgraded to Active Member once they refer and add 25 General Members to the party under their membership.
1
Register as General Member — complete the General Membership form and get your official Member ID.
2
Help 25 people join — guide others to register as General Members and collect their Member IDs.
3
Add their IDs in Member Area — login to your Member Area and use the "Upgrade to Active Member" section to enter their IDs one by one.
4
Auto-upgrade at 25 referrals — once you add 25 valid Member IDs, your account is automatically upgraded to Active Member with a new Active Member ID card.

Membership FAQ

You must be at least 18 years of age to register as a General Member or Volunteer with Jan Kalyan Party.
The minimum annual fee for General Membership is ₹10. You may choose to contribute more. Volunteer registration is completely free.
Membership is valid for 3 years, starting from 1st April and ending on 31st March of the third year. Renewal is required after the tenure ends.
First register as a General Member. Then help 25 people join as General Members and collect their Member IDs. Login to your Member Area, go to "Upgrade to Active Member", and enter each ID. Once you add all 25, you are automatically upgraded to Active Member with a new ID card.
After successful registration, your digital ID card is shown immediately in a popup. You can print it directly. You can also login to your Member Area at any time to view, download, and print your ID card again.
Yes. Your Aadhaar and personal details are securely encrypted and stored locally. They are never shared with third parties. We comply with India's data protection guidelines.
