Membership FAQ

No. Joining Jan Kalyan Party as a General Member, Active Member, or Volunteer is completely free of charge. We believe participation in democracy should be accessible to all.
You must be at least 18 years of age to register as a Member or Volunteer with Jan Kalyan Party, as per the Election Commission of India guidelines.
After successful registration, your digital ID card is generated instantly and displayed on screen. You can print it or download it directly. A physical card dispatch feature will be available soon.
A General Member gets basic party rights — ID card, newsletter, and voting rights in party elections. An Active Member gets everything in General membership plus campaign participation rights, direct access to party leadership, and eligibility for party positions.
